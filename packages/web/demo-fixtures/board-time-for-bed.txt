The sun sinks low, the day is done.
Time for bed, little one.

The puppy yawns inside his bed.
Time for bed, sleepyhead.

The kitten curls up soft and warm.
Time for bed, safe and warm.

The little baby elephant stretches out his trunk and quietly sighs.
Time for bed, close your eyes.

The birdie tucks beneath her wing.
Time for bed, everything.

The moon begins its luminous ascent.
Time for bed, the day is spent.

And now the whole wide world's asleep.
Time for bed. Not a peep.
